Simple Grilled Cheese Recipe

This is a simple grilled cheese recipe that you can make for a quick lunch when you're low on time. All you need is bread, butter, and cheese. With a combination like that, what's not to like! Not to mention, the ingredients are really cheap and should allow you to make lunches for at least a week.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of white bread
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 2 slices of american cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet to 275 degreed Farenheit and melt butter.

  2. Blace one slice of cheese on to pan, top with two slices of cheese, then top with other slice of bread.

  3. Flip Sandwich over in skiller once side is toasted and cheese is melted. Make sure to move this newly place down side around the skillet to soak up the remainder of the butter. Take sanwich off of the pan once both sides are toasted.

  4. Enjoy!
