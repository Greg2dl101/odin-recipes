Simple Beef Stroganoff!

This is a recipe for something I've yet to make myself but really enjoy. Beef stroganoff is not only cheap, but delicious and easy to make. Great meal to make for your family or friends during dinner night at your place!

Ingredients

  • 1 8-ounce package of egg noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 10.5 can of fat-freee condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Saute ground beef in a large skiller over medium heat until browned and crumblyl 5-10 minutes.

  3. At the same time, fill a large put with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to bite; 7-9 minutes. Drain and set aside.

  4. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef. Stir condensed soup and garlic powder into the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Remove beef from the heat. Add egg noodles and stir to combine. Stir in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.

  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
