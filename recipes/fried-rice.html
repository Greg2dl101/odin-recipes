Simple Fried Rice Recipe!

This is a simple fried rice recipe that I make quite often.

Ingredients

  • White short grain rice
  • Small white onion
  • Mixed veggies: corn, peas, carrots
  • Sesame oil
  • Eggs
  • MSG
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet Turn your skillet on medium high and pur sesame oil into the bottom of your skillet and add the onion, peas and carrots. Fry this until they are tender.

  2. Slide the veggies to the sideUse a spatula to scramble the eggs. Mix them together witht he veggies.

  3. Add the rice. Combine it with the veggies and egg mixture. Pour the soy sauce on top and stir until it is heated throughout. Make sure to seasonw ith MSG, salt and pepper!

  4. Enjoy!
